Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I fix a slow PC?

    A slow PC can be caused by several factors, such as low RAM, a fragmented hard drive, or too many background processes. Follow our step-by-step guide to clean up your system, uninstall unnecessary programs, and optimize your PC for better performance.

  • How can I remove viruses from my computer?

    To remove viruses, use a reliable antivirus program to scan and clean your system. If the issue persists, follow our detailed guide on manual virus removal and system recovery.

  • How do I upgrade my PC’s RAM?

    Upgrading your PC’s RAM is a simple process. First, check your motherboard’s compatibility, purchase the correct RAM, and follow our step-by-step tutorial to install it safely.

  • What should I do if my PC won’t turn on?

    If your PC won’t turn on, check the power supply, cables, and connections. If the issue persists, follow our troubleshooting guide to diagnose and fix the problem.
